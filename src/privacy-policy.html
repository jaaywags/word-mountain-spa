Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you use the Word Mountain mobile application (the "App").

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to assure you that Word Mountain is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from you. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your phone number
  • Your location data
  • Your device identifiers (such as IMEI or Advertising ID)
  • Your browsing history
  • Any data about your contacts or other apps you use

Usage Data (Anonymous)

We may collect anonymous usage data to understand how users interact with the App. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally. This may include information such as:

  • The number of games played
  • Statistics on wins, losses, and skips
  • General patterns in gameplay (without linking to individual users)

This anonymous usage data helps us to improve the App's functionality and user experience.

No Third-Party Sharing

Because we do not collect any personal information, we do not share any personal information with third parties. Any anonymous usage data we collect is used internally to improve the App.

Children's Privacy

Word Mountain is a general audience app and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at the email address provided below, and we will take steps to delete such information.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date at the bottom will be revised. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any updates.
